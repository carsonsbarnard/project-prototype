★★★★★

Author: Laura Hillenbrand

Description: Unbroken is an extraordinary story of survival, resilience, and redemption. This biography chronicles the life of Louis Zamperini, an Olympic runner who survived a plane crash in WWII, drifted at sea for 47 days, and endured two years as a Japanese prisoner of war.
